About

I am now a Ph.D. student in Computer Science at the University of California, Irvine. My research areas are entity resolution, knowledge discovery, distributed systems and machine learning. Currently, I focus on applying machine learning models to do fast record linkage or quick partitioning in dynamic scenarios.

Lab: Edge Lab

Advisor: Faisal Nawab
